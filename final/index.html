Addendum

  • The process of crafting the "Revenge of Lights Out" game was a journey filled with discovery, blending creativity with problem-solving.
  • Our main aim was to fashion an interactive and captivating game that presents an enjoyable challenge to players.

Creative choices:

  • Game Design: Opting for a 5x5 grid aimed to strike a balance between difficulty and accessibility, catering to players of various skill levels.
  • User Interface: Embracing a minimalist design ethos for the interface kept the gameplay at the forefront, aligning with contemporary design principles emphasizing simplicity and user-friendliness.
  • Feedback Mechanism: Employing a modal window to signal game completion aimed to offer players a clear and gratifying acknowledgment of their achievement.

challenges and Solutions:

  • Randomizing the Board: Initially challenging, implementing an effective board shuffling mechanism was achieved through the introduction of a randomized function, ensuring a fresh experience with each playthrough.
  • Ensuring Solvability: Guaranteeing the game remained solvable after shuffling required intricate adjustments to the shuffling algorithm, achieved through rigorous testing and refinement.

Lessons Learned:

  • User-Centric Design: Emphasizing the user experience emerged as pivotal, with simple, intuitive gameplay and interface design significantly enhancing player immersion.
  • Testing Importance: Thorough testing proved indispensable in identifying and resolving gameplay and usability issues, underscoring the critical role of testing in game development.
